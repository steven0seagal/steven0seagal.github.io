KINtaro Database

A comprehensive database for protein kinase-like (PKL) families, combining protein sequence information and annotations for more than 70 PKL families. The platform provides searchable resources including HMM models, 3D structure models, and sequence alignments.

Protein Family Neighborhood Analyzer - A statistical approach for exploring functional relationships between protein domains across different taxonomic levels. The tool leverages genomic neighborhood analysis for functional predictions.

Legionella Pan-Kinome Study

Comprehensive bioinformatics survey exploring the pan-kinome of Legionella bacteria, discovering 13 novel (pseudo)kinase families using advanced computational approaches. Catalog available through the KINtaro database.
